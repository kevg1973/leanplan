LeanPlan tells users exactly what to eat and do — no blank screen, no decision fatigue.

AI-generated personalised meal plans

A full week of meals built around your diet, allergies, calorie targets and what's in your pantry. Generated in seconds.

Injury-aware workout programmes

Filters exercises by your actual kit and skips anything your back or knees can't handle. Fully personalised from day one.
